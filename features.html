Features

Discover the features of The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.

There is no kingdom. There are no recollections. After a century-long slumber, Link awakens alone in a world he no longer recognises. Now, the great hero must go across a vast and hazardous realm to reclaim his memories before Hyrule is permanently lost.

With only what he can scavenge, Link sets out to discover answers and the resources he needs to survive.

Explore the enormous world around you.

Explore Hyrule's landscape in whatever way you like. Climb towers and mountain summits in pursuit of new locations, then forge your own path and venture into the wild.

Fight for Your Kingdom

When Link first awakens, he is unarmed and defenceless. Using his brains and cunning, he may acquire over 180 different weapons for warfare. These products will eventually wear out, therefore he must continually be on the hunt for new ones.

Fighting involves more than simply weaponry. Link will also need to learn a range of various attacks, such as high-flying bow shots and explosives, in order to succeed.

Explore Hyrule. The Sheikah Slate includes a map of Hyrule to help Link's journey. It also has the ability to locate points of interest from a distance.

Get organised. The Sheikah Slate allows Link to manage his adventure by organizing items in the Inventory and charting tasks in the Adventure Log.

Download Runes. In some Shrines, Link can download Runes to gain new powers that will help him overcome puzzles and uncover secret areas.
